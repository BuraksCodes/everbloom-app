## Description (4000 chars max)

Everbloom is your personal anxiety and panic support companion — designed to help you breathe, ground yourself, and find calm whenever you need it most.

Whether you're in the middle of a panic attack, feeling overwhelmed by anxiety, or simply want to build better mental wellness habits, Everbloom is here for you 24/7.

**🆘 PANIC RELIEF — Your Emergency Calm Button**
When anxiety peaks, tap the Panic Relief button for instant, guided support. Everbloom walks you through proven grounding techniques — the 5-4-3-2-1 sensory method, breathing exercises, and calming affirmations — with soothing voice guidance and ambient rain sounds to help bring you back to the present moment.

**🌬️ BREATHING EXERCISES**
Six science-backed breathing techniques to reduce anxiety and stress:
• Box Breathing — used by Navy SEALs to stay calm under pressure
• 4-7-8 Breathing — the natural tranquilizer for your nervous system
• Diaphragmatic Breathing — deep belly breaths for instant relaxation
• Resonance Breathing — balance your heart rate and calm your mind
• Pursed Lip Breathing — slow your breath and reduce tension
• Alternate Nostril Breathing — an ancient technique for mental clarity

**🎵 AMBIENT SOUNDS**
Six calming soundscapes to help you focus, sleep, or unwind — rain, ocean waves, forest, white noise, and more. Set a sleep timer and drift off peacefully.

**📓 GUIDED JOURNAL**
Express your thoughts freely in a beautiful, private journal. Use AI-powered prompts to help you start when you don't know what to write. Your entries are encrypted and private — only you can see them.

**😊 MOOD TRACKING**
Check in with yourself daily and track your emotional patterns over time. Visual insights help you understand your triggers and celebrate your progress.

**🧘 MEDITATION LIBRARY**
Guided meditation sessions for anxiety relief, sleep, stress reduction, and morning calm. Each session features gentle narration and ambient music.

**🤖 BLOOM AI CHAT**
Talk to Bloom, your empathetic AI wellness companion. Bloom listens without judgment, offers coping strategies, and helps you process difficult emotions — available anytime, day or night.

**🗺️ THERAPIST FINDER**
Ready to take the next step? Find licensed therapists near you with ratings, specialties, and contact info — all within the app.

---

Everbloom is built with love for everyone who has ever felt overwhelmed, anxious, or alone in their struggle. You are not alone. Help is always one tap away.

*Everbloom is not a substitute for professional medical or psychological treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services or call/text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).*
