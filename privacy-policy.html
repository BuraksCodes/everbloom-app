Everbloom Privacy Policy

Anxiety & Panic Support App

Effective date: March 22, 2026

Your privacy matters deeply to us. Everbloom is built for moments of vulnerability — and we take that responsibility seriously. This policy explains clearly and honestly what data we collect, how we use it, and what we never do with it.

📋

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address and display name, collected when you sign up via Firebase Authentication.
  • Journal entries: Text you write in the app. Stored privately in your Firebase account — only you can access them.
  • Mood check-ins: Daily mood ratings and optional notes you log. Stored in your Firebase account.
  • Profile photo: Stored locally on your device only. Never uploaded to our servers.
  • Anonymous usage data: Crash reports to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is included.
🚫

What We Never Do

  • We never sell your data to third parties.
  • We never share your journal entries or mood data with anyone.
  • We never collect precise location data.
  • We never use your data for advertising.
  • We never read or store your Bloom AI conversations on our servers.
💡

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your experience (greetings, mood history, journal).
  • To send optional reminder notifications — only if you enable them.
  • To process subscription purchases via Apple's App Store and RevenueCat.
  • To respond to support requests if you contact us.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase's infrastructure, protected by Firebase security rules so only you can access your own data. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest.

Your profile photo is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

🗑️

Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app: Profile → Delete Account.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account removes all journal entries, mood history, and account information immediately and cannot be undone.
👶

Children's Privacy

Everbloom is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

⚖️

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data via the in-app account deletion feature.
  • Opt out of notifications at any time in your device Settings.
📝

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
