Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Everbloom → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open Everbloom → go to the Premium screen → tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase.

The voice guidance isn't playing — what do I do?

Check that your phone is not on silent mode. Go to Settings → Everbloom and confirm audio and notification permissions are enabled. An internet connection is required for voice guidance.

Is my journal private?

Yes. Your journal entries are stored securely and are only accessible to you. We never share your personal data with third parties.

Can I use Everbloom offline?

Breathing exercises are available offline. Voice guidance, Bloom AI chat, and the therapist finder require an internet connection.

Is Everbloom a replacement for therapy?

No — Everbloom is a wellness support tool, not a medical device or therapy service. We warmly encourage all users to seek professional mental health support when needed.

About Everbloom

Everbloom is a personal anxiety and panic support companion designed to help you find calm in moments of stress and build long-term emotional resilience. Our tools are grounded in evidence-based techniques including CBT, mindfulness, and breathwork — offered with warmth, and without judgment.
